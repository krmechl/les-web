OUR HISTORY, OUR MISSION…

Since 1968, Carmen V. D’Ambrosio has been involved in healthcare—with national and international experience. In 1976, he incorporated to create a business model that would provide service for a broad range of needs within the diagnostic laboratory setting.

Those services addressed areas from the manufacturing of diagnostic reagents for blood bank and microbiology use, to the creation of the first courier service exclusively set up for the transport of blood and blood products.

It was 1981 when Mr. D’Ambrosio recognized an opportunity for the industry to outsource transportation requirements—and realize improved service and reduced costs. With his in-depth knowledge of the field, Mr. D’Ambrosio’s detailed application to the Pennsylvania Utilities Commission (PUC) was approved, and on October 30, 1981, he was awarded a license—the first in Pennsylvania, the first in the tri-state area, and one of the first in the nation—to exclusively transport blood, specimens, and related products under the same standards mandated for hospitals and clinical laboratories. And, even with current deregulation standards, Bio-Test Express has remained the preferred choice of the region’s doctors, laboratory technicians, and hospital systems.

Bio-Test Express is superior because our objective is defined by a clear mission:

 

  • The purpose of Bio-Test Express transportation services is to provide a highly specialized company made up of professional and caring employees who have made it their mission to receive, transport, and deliver blood-related materials.

 

  • The manner in which this is accomplished is done by employing the finest people available, acquiring the best in equipment and material, and by providing special orientation as to the care and handling of the products we carry. This is all done to ensure and guarantee the integrity of the blood and related specimens Bio-Test Express transports.

Today, Bio-Test Express continues to be guided by the leadership of Carmen V. D’Ambriosio, Jr. Headquartered in Gibsonia, PA, it’s a private, family-run Company. The Company's President is Carmen L. D’Ambrosio—a graduate of the West Virginia University, with a degree in Business Administration and Accounting. He brings with him more than two decades of experience in assorted healthcare operations, and a management approach that's personable, engaged, and admired by all employees.

The Company continues to expand and improve upon the foundation of its initial success. Now, with more than 100 employees carrying out hundreds of assignments per day, and proven protocols and policies in place, Bio-Test Express is poised to partner with Healthcare organizations of any size and scope.
