LOCAL AND REGIONAL... NATIONAL WHEN NECESSARY.

Bio-Test Express’ Corporate Offices are located just outside Pittsburgh, in Gibsonia, Pennsylvania. From this convenient location in the North Hills, we serve the Greater Pittsburgh area, encompassing a 100-mile radius on a typical day. Currently, the Company has Transportation Hubs in Huntington and Morgantown (West Virginia), Johnstown and Reading (Pennsylvania), and Norfolk and Roanoke (Virginia).

Our network grows with our Client base. And, in cases where a client’s network grows, e.g., integrating satellite hospitals and healthcare facilities, we’ll branch out as well...either through our existing Hubs, or by adding Hubs. In the instance that nation-wide transportation becomes necessary,Bio-Test maintains a roster of Partner Couriers that will carry out our standard of excellence across the country.

With our Hub Personnel and national Partner Couriers, we demand the application of Bio-Test’sstringent protocols for professionalism, safety, punctuality, and parcel tracking. And on a continual basis, Senior Management is in contact with their staff. Everything from daily reports, to problem-resolution, to hiring, is monitored to ensure that operations run smooth and on-time.
