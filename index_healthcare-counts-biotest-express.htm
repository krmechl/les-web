Equipment & Safety

  • Bio-Test has a variety of vehicles to meet our customers’ demands...from PT Cruisers and minivans, to Ford cargo vans and 16-foot straight trucks. All are Company-owned and maintained, and clearly marked with the Bio-Test logo.
  • Every vehicle is equipped with a standard Spill Kit, in the event that a Courier needs to contain a spill or leak of biohazardous material.
  • At the start of their shift, Bio-Test drivers are issued a Nextel two-way communication system with cell phone capabilities.
  • Depending on the customer’s requirements, Bio-Test has a system of bar-coding and electronic scanning of delivery items, to provide real-time tracking.
  • All Couriers carry customized Bio-Test specimen transport systems. By using proper combinations of cold packs and dry ice, we’re able to ensure that the proper temperature of the specimen is maintained for the duration of the delivery.
  • When necessary, Bio-Test supplies metal Lock Boxes for the client to place outside their office. Our Couriers can then pick up specimens after their offices have closed for the day.

Account Management

Total Customer Satisfaction is the ultimate goal. Yet, with over-the-road, point-to-point deliveries, obstacles are an everyday reality. To ensure our clients’ confidence in us, we have, in place, time-tested account management standards and procedures for the resolution of complaints and unforeseen problems.

A Customer Service Representative is assigned to each account, and he/she is their clients’ first contact on any issue that needs addressed. Among those issues are:

  • Problem Resolution. The CSR is empowered to address customer complaints, and is required to report to Senior Management, who will follow up with the client to make certain of their satisfaction.
  • Route Management. Routes are planned for daily, weekly, and monthly requirements. Using information provided by our clients, along with experience and detailed study, we ensure our Couriers follow the most efficient, cost-effective routes possible. STATs are received and dispatched by Bio-Test’s in-house call center. All STATs are given control numbers that correspond to a timed requirement of two-hour delivery from the receipt of the call. Signature confirmation is required at both pick-up points and delivery destinations, mandated for both regulatory and control purposes.
